Recognition notes

Helpful reminders before you start recording.

  • Browser permission is required before microphone capture starts.
  • Continuous mode keeps recognition active across longer sessions.
  • Interim results update in real time and may change before they finalize.
  • Speech recognition support is more limited than speech synthesis across browsers.
